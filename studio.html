Who are we

We make the weird stuff you've never seen before.

Bad Rabbit is a One person Indie animation studio. Right now, that one person is me, and I'm on a mission to share the most wacky, original stories with anyone who will watch. The kind of stories the big money bags would never greenlight. All because they're too weird and bold, and made with too much heart.

As of 2026, I'm working on pushing out our own stories first before opening up to outside projects. Every idea here comes froma real place, and I'm not willing to rush that.

I'm always looking to bring more people who share this passion. We're not a corporation, We're all in this together.
