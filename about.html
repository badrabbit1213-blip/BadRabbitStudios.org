Who Am I

Hey, I'm Bad Rabbit

I'm an animator, artist, and writer who's been building worlds on the internet for as long as I can remember. Bad Rabbit Studio is the home I made for everything I create. A place where stories can exist without being watered down or compromise because some big money guy says it's "too risky".

I started this because I genuinely believe there are stories worth telling that nobody else is telling. Stories that are weird, bold, and made with real love for the craft. The FunTime Show is the one I've been carrying around for 5 years now, and I'm finally ready to bring it to life.

This studio is everything I've worked toward. It's scary and exciting and I wouldn't have it any other way. Thanks for being here. Seriously.

My Journey
2020
Where Scribbles Started
Quarintine killed any social skills I had. I lost many friends and went through a dark time. I began reading comics online and got knees deep into the Springtrap and Delilah comic series. I began drawing small fanart snippets that I never dared to share online, beating myself up for the fact that my drawings didn't look exactly like the comic.
2021
Scribbles...Doodles.
After a year of using someone else's drawings for my tiktok videos and edits, I did a complete 180. I made multiple posts saying I was going to make tiktoks of my drawings, I was quitting my old style. This costed me everything, I no longer had 40k followers. While my profile may say that, I lost them all. Except for one, Chimkin.
2022-2024
Doodles and now Dreams
I began drawing daily non-stop. I developed so many story ideas; Some that never touched the light of day, some that are now getting full scripts. Throughout all of this,Chimkin. continously supported me. Hyping up every story board, every sketch, every idea, every blurt I spat out. Without her, I would've stopped a long time ago. Thanks to her, I now have confidence in my weird and abstract story ideas.
2025
Research and ideas
I soon began looking into the logistics of running a Animation/Entertainment studio. While putting this research in, I was juggling college classes, highschool classes, german classes, commissions, partnerships, collaborations, story writing, ideas to gain an audience, and so much more. This posed many difficulties, however, instead of slowing down I sped up. I doubled my German practice, I stayed up nights studing animation practices, I read more story books to take notes on my own writing. I learned that the more you do, the more you get. And nothing was going to stop me and my goals.
NOW - 2026
Dreams are now goals, soaring.
School began to slow down, German is easing up, and finances are growing. After learning about all of the things I would need, I acted. I soon after bought my website's domain, using code from my Neocities Website to edit my official website.
